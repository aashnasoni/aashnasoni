Illinois

Land of Lincoln

Attractions

Chicago is definitely the city to visit during your trip to Illinois. The Willis Sky Deck has a full view of the city, up to over four states and the beautiful Lake Michigan. The Bean is also something you don't want to miss, take some fun photos with your friends.

Food

The Infamous Deep Dish Pizza is just to die for! Be sure to visit Beatnik at the River, a personal favourite restaurant that serves American food in a very aesthetic setting.

Hidden Gems

After spending the rest of the day admiring work at the Art Institute of Chicago, be sure to visit the state parks and go for a fun camping trip, there are so many new areas to explore!
