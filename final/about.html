About This Experience

Feeling isolated and stuck at home?

Want a personalized itinerary for your travels?

Input your email so that we can send you a plan for where to go depending what you're interested in.

The COVID-19 Pandemic has definitely set us all back many ways, but also in our chance to explore the world and learn of new cultures. This website has been created in order to allow people to virtually explore different places in the US and learn more about the food, attractions and hidden spots they can visit.


There are various tabs to explore and interact with different pins on the map, while there are only some states that have been covered, the website is still under development and we will soon have more options and information. We also have the option for users to create their own travel bucket list and have a record of the things they want to do and have already done in various parts of the world.
